About Gee Music

The story behind the music.

Who is Gee Music?

Gee Music is a Kenyan singer, songwriter and producer passionate about creating music that connects with people through emotion, storytelling and authentic African sounds.

His catalogue blends Afro Fusion, Drill, Afro Pop and Rhumba, delivering energetic performances and memorable melodies that continue to grow his audience.

From songs like Habibi, Njoki Remix, Steady Love and Tell Me Now, Gee Music continues to build a unique sound and inspire fans across different platforms.
